Doraemon

Doraemon is a robotic cat from the future who has come back in time to help a young boy named Nobita Nobi. With his gadgets from the future, Doraemon helps Nobita navigate his everyday life and challenges. Doraemon is known for his blue color, round face, and his red collar.

Nobita Nobi

Nobita Nobi is a kind-hearted but lazy boy who struggles with school and sports. His life takes a turn for the better with the help of Doraemon. He is known for his glasses and his constant battle against his own clumsiness.

Shizuka Minamoto

Shizuka Minamoto is a smart and kind girl who is Nobita's classmate and close friend. She is known for her gentle nature and love of playing the piano. Nobita has a crush on her, and she often helps him with his problems.

Suneo Honekawa

Suneo Honekawa is one of Nobita's classmates and is known for being a bit of a bully. He often shows off and teases Nobita and his friends, but deep down, he values their friendship.

Takeshi Gouda (Gian)

Takeshi Gouda, commonly known as Gian, is known for his strong personality and love for singing. Despite his tough exterior, he is a good friend and helps Nobita and the others when they need him.
